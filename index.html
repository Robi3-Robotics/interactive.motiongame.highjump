👉 3-LINE MANUAL CALIBRATION:

  • Drag the horizontal lines on the canvas to match physical reference markers in your room:
  • 💗 2.0m Line: 2.0 meters above the floor
  • 💛 1.5m Line: 1.5 meters above the floor
  • 💙 1.0m Line: 1.0 meter above the floor
  • Once aligned, click **Lock Calibration** to lock the visual scale.

3. Player Focus

Tracking all motions in the full camera feed. Ideal for single-athlete setups.

Quick Guide

  1. Input your height and stand about 2.5 - 3.5 meters (8 - 12 feet) from the camera.
  2. Click **Start Calibration** and raise your jump hand straight up.
  3. Stand still during the 3-second countdown.
  4. Take a step back, coil, and **EXPLODE** upwards reaching as high as possible!
